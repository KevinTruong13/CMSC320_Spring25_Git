Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: It enables collaboration by allowing multiple people to work on a
repo together with syncing functionality.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science can be used in fields like marketing by studying the 
spending habits and trends of consumers.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines changes between branches, 
preserving the history of both branches with a merge commit.
Rebasing adds the commits of one branch to the end of the recipient, which makes the git log
linear.
